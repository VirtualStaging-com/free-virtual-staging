About this tool

What is free virtual staging?

Virtual staging is the practice of digitally adding furniture and decor to property photos. Traditional services charge $20 to $200 per photo and take hours to days. This tool uses OpenAI's gpt-image-2 model to do the same thing in your browser, free, using your own API key.

Bring your own key

Pay OpenAI directly at their published rates (~$0.19 per photo at high quality). No middleman, no markup, no subscription. Your API key stays in your browser.

9 furniture styles

Modern, Scandinavian, Transitional, Farmhouse, Coastal, Industrial, Traditional, Rustic, Mid-Century Modern. Each one tuned for real estate photo staging.

Bulk processing

Upload up to 25 photos at once. Stage a full listing in one batch. Download each result individually as a PNG.

No data leaves your browser

Photos go directly from your browser to OpenAI. Nothing is stored on a server. Close the tab and everything is gone except your downloaded files.

Honest about limitations

AI image editing is impressive but variable. Some photos will look great. Some will need re-runs. For MLS-ready output, use a designer-led service.
